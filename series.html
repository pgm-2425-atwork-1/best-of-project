My favorite

Series

Last year I said Netflix kept on releasing interesting series, but I think I cancelled and restarted my subscription twice this year. There was a definite lack of good series, and I had to look elsewhere to find good content. I even re-watched Weeds and Mad Men because I was searching for something good. Luckily, Apple launched Apple TV+ with two fun series, and at the end of the year, the Witcher landed on Netflix. So all in all, it wasn’t that bad.
